Privacy Policy for Kid Star

Last Updated: February 8, 2026

Introduction

Kid Star ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of your children. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our personalized children's story generation app.

Our Privacy-First Approach

Kid Star is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. We believe that your family's data should remain yours, which is why we've built the app to operate entirely on your device using Apple Intelligence.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Kid Star does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share:

  • Character names or any personally identifiable information you enter
  • Story content generated by the app
  • User preferences or settings
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Contacts, photos, or other device data
  • Any information about you or your children

How the App Works

On-Device Processing

  • All story generation happens directly on your device using Apple Intelligence (on-device large language models)
  • Character names, story preferences, and generated stories are stored locally in your device's secure storage
  • No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties
  • Your device's processing power and Apple's on-device AI handle all story creation

Local Data Storage

  • Stories and preferences are stored using SwiftData in your device's local database
  • Data is protected by your device's security features (encryption, Face ID/Touch ID, passcode)
  • Only you can access this data through the app
  • Data remains on your device and is included in your iCloud backup (if enabled) but is not accessible to us

Third-Party Services

Kid Star does NOT use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Third-party data processors
  • Cloud-based AI services
  • External servers for any functionality

In-App Purchases

If you choose to upgrade to Premium:

  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not receive or store your payment information
  • Apple's privacy policy governs payment transactions
  • We only receive confirmation of purchase status from Apple (no personal details)

Children's Privacy

Kid Star is specifically designed for use by parents and caregivers to create stories for children. We comply with children's privacy laws including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act):

  • The app does not collect any information from children
  • The app does not enable children to share information
  • There is no social networking or user-generated content sharing
  • No data about children leaves your device
  • Parents maintain complete control over all story content and character information

Data Security

Your data security is paramount:

  • All data remains on your device
  • Data is protected by iOS/iPadOS/macOS security features
  • No network transmission of personal information
  • No external servers or databases that could be compromised

iCloud Backup

If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your device:

  • Your stories and app data may be included in your encrypted iCloud backup
  • This backup is controlled by you and protected by Apple's security
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data
  • See Apple's privacy policy for information about iCloud security

Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app
  • Deletion: Delete individual stories or all app data by deleting the app
  • Export: Export stories as PDF files that remain on your device
  • No Account: No account or registration is required, so there's no profile to manage

Email Support

If you contact us via email for support:

  • We will only use your email address to respond to your inquiry
  • Support emails are deleted after your issue is resolved
  • We do not add email addresses to marketing lists
  • We do not share email addresses with third parties

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in:

  • The app's features
  • Legal requirements
  • Best practices

When we make changes:

  • The "Last Updated" date will be revised
  • Significant changes will be communicated through the app
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance

Legal Compliance

Kid Star complies with:

  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable privacy laws

As we do not collect any personal data, most data subject rights and obligations do not apply, as there is no data to access, correct, or delete on our end.

Summary

  • Your family's privacy is completely protected
